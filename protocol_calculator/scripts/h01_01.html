    Let's suppose, that it is necessary to prepare 700ml of 10M AcONH4, but in the table below there is only 10ml volume.

    • Insert "700" in the right-up field (it is green here).
    • Push on "Calculate" button (on the orange field) or just click somewhere outside the field.
    • The table with the necessary volume will be obtained.

    1. it is does not matter, what to insert: "700", "700ml", "700L" or "700 buckets", in any case it will be treated as "700ml";
