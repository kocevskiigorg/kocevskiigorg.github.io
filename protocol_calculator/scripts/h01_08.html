Help for units of measurement

Suppose, you want to know how many of "µCi" are in 5MBq.

Insert "5" in the bottom field and select "MBq" from the list of units. Push "assign original value" button and "5MBq" will appear in the upper field. Now; select "µCi", ad you will see, that "5MBq"="135.14µCi". If to select "cpm": "5MBq"="297297297.3cpm".
